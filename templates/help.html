Help Center

Welcome to the Ticko Help Center. We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have while browsing, booking, or managing tickets for live events.

1. Getting Started

To start using Ticko:

  • Create an account by clicking here and filling in your details.
  • Browse events by category—Concerts, Movies, Live Shows, Sports—and select the event you want to attend.
  • Add tickets to your cart and proceed to secure checkout.

2. Managing Your Tickets

  • Access your booked tickets from your My Tickets page.
  • Download or print your tickets for entry to events.
  • If an event is canceled or rescheduled, you’ll receive notifications via email.

3. Payment and Refunds

  • All payments are processed securely through our platform.
  • Refunds and cancellations are handled according to the event’s specific policies. Please review the event terms when booking.
  • If you experience any payment issues, contact our support team immediately.

4. Account Issues

  • Forgot your password? Use the Reset Password option on the login page.
  • Need to update your personal information? Visit Account Settings after logging in.
  • For account security concerns, contact our support team immediately.

5. Contact Support

If you can’t find what you’re looking for, our support team is here to help:

  • Email: support@ticko.com
  • Phone: +254 721 718874

We’re committed to making your live event experience smooth and enjoyable. Thank you for choosing Ticko!
